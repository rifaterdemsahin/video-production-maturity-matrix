- **Finding:** 8-15 min videos maximize ad revenue AND retention for tech content
- **Finding:** <5 min videos better for Shorts repurposing
- **Action:** Target 10-12 min for main videos, create Shorts from highlights

### Upload Frequency vs Quality
- **Finding:** Consistent 1/week outperforms irregular 3-4/week bursts
- **Source:** Creator analysis, 2025
- **Action:** Cadence first, then quality
