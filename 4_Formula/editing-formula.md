## ‚úÖ Editing Checklist

### Cut & Assembly
- [ ] Remove all "ums", long pauses (>0.5s), false starts
- [ ] Jump cuts tightened (max 0.3s gap between cuts)
- [ ] B-roll covers every technical explanation
- [ ] Screen recordings crisp and readable

### Audio
- [ ] EQ applied: highpass at 80Hz, presence boost at 3kHz
- [ ] Noise reduction applied
- [ ] Loudness normalized to -14 LUFS (YouTube standard)
- [ ] Music ducked to -30dB under voice

### Visual
- [ ] Color grade applied (saved preset)
- [ ] Lower thirds: name + topic at intro
- [ ] Captions enabled (auto via DaVinci or Whisper)
- [ ] Intro bumper: 2-3 seconds max
- [ ] Outro: subscribe CTA + end screen cards (20 seconds)

### Export Settings
```
Format: MP4 / H.264
Resolution: 1920x1080
Bitrate: 16 Mbps (variable)
Audio: AAC 320kbps
```
